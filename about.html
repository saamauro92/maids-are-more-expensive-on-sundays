Alicia I. Ciocca,

was born in Buenos Aires, Argentina, on the 1st of September, 1956. As a Teacher of English, she worked for many years at primary and secondary schools in her home town before founding the family-run company Global Studies along with one of her six sons. The main activity at Global Studies has been recruiting students from Argentina and accompanying them to do courses in Bournemouth, England.

As a result of her wide experience on her group trips to Bournemouth schools Alicia feels this place -she loves so much- has been the inspiring force and hence the setting for her novel and the fictional characters.

At present she is an Educational Consultant at Global Studies (www.globalstudies.es) and goes on recruiting and accompanying groups of students to do courses in Bournemouth twice a year.
