About the Book

This novel takes place in Bournemouth in contemporary times, when a student from Peru comes to England to do a course of English and to fulfil her godfather's will.

By leaving Jessica his highly unusual legacy, uncle Luis Alberto is confident that she will do her duty and that he will be able to keep his promise.

Now, in the beautiful area of Bournemouth and totally immersed in a different culture, Jessica's heart and mind open up to new horizons.

She stays in a guest house called "The Renaissance" where every single new acquaintance she meets imprints their own personal story.

While the protagonists share daily life, the more they relate, the more they reveal their secrets. When their voices are heard, truths are closer...
